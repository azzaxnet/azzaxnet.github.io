About Me

This is Håkan!

👋 I'm Håkan Nilsson, your friendly Swedish UX/UI designer based in the picturesque Basque Country, Spain. With over three years of crafting digital experiences, I'm all about making your online journey smoother and more delightful.

🇸🇪 My roots may be Swedish, but my work knows no borders. I've honed my skills over a decade as a remote worker, proving that distance is no match for dedication. I'm like a Swedish clock—humble, well-organized, and always curious to learn and explore

🌍 When I'm not immersed in user needs, pixels and interfaces, I'm exploring far and wide or enjoying quality time with my family. Oh, and did I mention I'm an avid runner? Because, let's face it, running is the best way to chase those creative ideas

🧑🏽‍💻 So, whether it's revamping your user experience, running with new concepts, or simply sharing travel stories, I'm here for it. Let's create, connect, and make digital magic happen!
