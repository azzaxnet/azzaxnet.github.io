About me

Hej!

My name is Håkan Nilsson. I’m a Swede living in the Basque Country, Spain. After travelling and working worldwide for many years I gained experience in a variety of sectors. Having settled down, I’m now in a change of career.

With a profound interest in all things design and beautiful, but with a practical and usability point of view, I’m progressing into the field of UX design. Through putting in hours and hours of studies and practical work, I’ve gained a strong knowledge of the mindset of user-centred design which is an advantage both for the users and for the business behind the product. My vast international experience has taught me how to deal with people from different background and cultures, as well as to open my mind and see the importance of working hard to see a product not only through your own eyes, but also through the eyes of the client.
