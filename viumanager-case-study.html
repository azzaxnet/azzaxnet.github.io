Case Study: ViuManager

Managing Digital Displays

This case study provides an in-depth exploration of the design journey behind ViuManager, an application designed for managing digital displays. In my role as the UX/UI designer, I played a central part in this two-month project, developed for Viu, a daughter company of All Binary. ViuManager represents a pivotal step for Viu in creating a comprehensive system encompassing both physical displays and accompanying software. The challenge was to design a versatile system capable of managing various types of displays.

Project Details

Project Name ViuManager
Customer Viu
My Role UX/UI Designer
Project Duration 2 Months

Project Background

ViuManager represented a significant milestone for Viu, enabling the creation of a comprehensive system offering both physical displays and accompanying software. This application was designed to manage a wide array of digital displays, making it a versatile solution for Viu's clients.

Methodology

Our approach to this project centered around user-centered design principles, ensuring that the interface catered to the needs of managing diverse digital displays. The goal was to deliver a user-friendly and clear interface that met client expectations.

The Problem

The primary challenge of this project was to design an interface capable of managing various types of digital displays, ensuring ease of use and clarity for clients. The goal was to create a system that allowed clients to seamlessly control and interact with their digital displays.

The Process

Research and Client Engagement: The project began with extensive research and client discussions to understand the specific requirements for managing different types of digital displays. This research provided the foundation for the subsequent design phase.

Design and Iteration: Based on research findings, I initiated the design process, creating wireframes, low-fidelity prototypes, and high-fidelity mock-ups. Continuous feedback from clients and stakeholders played a crucial role in refining the design.

User Testing: The interface underwent rigorous user testing with Viu's important clients to ensure usability and clarity. Their feedback helped fine-tune the design for optimal user experience.

Final Outcome

The ViuManager project resulted in the successful creation of a user-friendly and clear interface for managing digital displays. The design met client expectations, and Viu's important clients found the system to be clean and easy to navigate. ViuManager has enabled Viu to offer a holistic system that includes both physical displays and software management.

Lessons Learned

Versatility: Designing for a versatile system requires a deep understanding of different display types and user needs.

Client Engagement: Close collaboration and feedback from clients are invaluable for ensuring that the design aligns with their expectations and needs.

User-Centered Design: Prioritizing usability and clarity is fundamental in creating interfaces that are easy to navigate and use.

In closing, the ViuManager project exemplified the successful creation of a versatile interface for managing digital displays, marking a significant step in Viu's vision for a comprehensive display and software system.
